Stop wasting time on **repetitive work**. Leave the boring tasks to AI and gain space for strategic decisions and your company's growth.

What is AI Automation and Who is it For?

AI automation is the process of using artificial intelligence and software tools to perform tasks that were previously done manually. It's not just about simple data transfers, but about intelligent information processing, decision-making, and coordinating complex processes.

It is ideal for **any company** that:

  • Repeatedly processes data (orders, invoices, emails).
  • Has manual processes that are prone to errors.
  • Wants to save its employees' time and resources.
  • Needs to respond faster to customer requests.
  • Is looking for innovative ways to get ahead of the competition.

How Can We Help You with AI Automation?

We are experts in connecting systems and automating processes. We use tools like Make (formerly Integromat) and n8n, which allow us to create robust and scalable automations without complex programming.

Make (formerly Integromat)

Make is a visual tool that allows you to connect virtually any application and automate data flows. From automatically sending emails after a new e-shop order to synchronizing your CRM with marketing tools – Make handles it efficiently and reliably.

  • Intuitive visual interface.
  • A huge number of supported applications.
  • Flexible scenarios for complex processes.

n8n (Node.js Workflow Automation)

n8n is a powerful open-source workflow automation tool that offers greater flexibility and control for specific requirements. It is ideal for advanced users and situations where deeper customization or local installation is needed.

  • Open-source solution with full control.
  • Option for local installation for sensitive data.
  • High flexibility and customizability.
