Ozobot Robots
for Schools

A fun and interactive path to programming for every child.

Why are Ozobots ideal for schools?

In today's world, it's crucial for children to not only use technology but also understand how it works. Ozobots are designed for exactly this purpose. Thanks to their simplicity and playfulness, they can get even complete beginners excited about programming and immediately show how code comes to life in the real world.

Fun that Educates

Programming Ozobots is designed so that anyone can do it. Children can start by drawing color codes on paper (with markers) and gradually move to block-based programming in the intuitive OzoBlockly graphical environment, which resembles assembling a puzzle. This smooth transition ensures that no one gets lost and everyone experiences a sense of accomplishment.
