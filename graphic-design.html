Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to our clients' most common questions and concerns

What file formats will I receive?

We deliver files in the most common formats like JPG, PNG (with transparent background), SVG (for vector graphics), PDF (for print), and, if needed, source files like AI or PSD.

Can I request revisions?

Yes, most of our graphic packages include a certain number of revisions to ensure your complete satisfaction. The number of revisions is always specified in the quote.

How long does a typical project take?

The project duration depends on its complexity and scope. Smaller projects (e.g., business cards) can take 3-5 days, larger projects (e.g., logo design) 5-10 days. We will always inform you of the exact timeframe in the offer.

Do you also offer printing services?

We do not offer printing services directly, but all our graphic designs are professionally prepared in the required formats for any print shop. We are happy to recommend trusted partners.

How does the payment work?

We usually require a deposit (50% of the total amount) before starting work and the remainder upon approval of the final design and file delivery. Payment details are always agreed upon individually.
