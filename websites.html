Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I even need a website? I already have Facebook.

Your website is your digital business card that you fully control. Facebook is a great tool, but:

  • Not all customers use social media
  • Your business looks more professional with your own domain
  • Google finds your website easier than a Facebook page
  • You own your website—no one can take it away
Why are your prices so low?

We achieve low prices through modern tools and efficient processes. You don’t pay for unnecessary meetings and bureaucracy—just real work. Every website has a unique custom design with professional SEO setup.

Are there any monthly fees?

No. The listed prices are one-time for website creation. The only ongoing costs are the annual domain fee (~€20/year) and optional email. We can set up free hosting via GitHub Pages.

Will customers find me on Google?

Yes! Today, 93% of people search for services on Google. Every site includes full SEO setup—meta tags, descriptions, structured URLs, sitemap, Google Search Console connection.
