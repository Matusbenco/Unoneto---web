Case Study: OMBRA D'ORO

How We Transformed an AI Vision into a Premium Logo

We are excited to present one of our recent projects, which was as challenging as it was thrilling. We worked for the client Chicessence s.r.o., who is preparing to launch a new, premium e-commerce store for women's fashion under the name OMBRA D'ORO.

The client came to us with a clear vision—an impressive logo design that was originally generated using artificial intelligence (AI). Our task was to "bring to life" this complex digital concept and transform it into a professional, vector logo (redrawn into curves) that could be used in practice—from the web to print.

The Challenge: When the Brief Scares (Almost) Everyone Away

Since we didn't have a free graphic designer on our internal team at the time, we started looking for external partners. And this is where we hit reality.

The logo had extremely complex curves and fine details that AI can design, but for a graphic designer, they represent hours of precise work. The scenario was almost always the same: we contacted numerous designers, essentially from all over the world. Everyone was interested in the job... until they saw the brief itself. After seeing it, most of them backed out of the work due to the high difficulty and complexity of the curves.

The Solution Named Zooba

Our philosophy at Unoneto is simple: "Have solutions, or find them." And so, we kept searching.

Finally, we managed to connect with an exceptionally talented graphic designer, Zooba. Zooba handled this difficult task brilliantly. While dozens of others hesitated, she got to work and delivered the finished, redrawn designs in an incredible 3 days. (Although, as she admitted with a smile, she probably didn't get much sleep during those days!)

The Result: Exceeding Expectations

The result is not just one logo, but five refined variations that perfectly capture the elegant and premium character of the OMBRA D'ORO brand.

We are proud that we could deliver exactly what Chicessence s.r.o. needed, even if the path to get there wasn't straightforward. Everyone is extremely satisfied with the final result—and so are we.

Have a Vision? We'll Find the Way.

This project is a perfect example of our approach. If you also have ideas, specific requirements, or even an "impossible" brief, believe that with us, you will successfully turn them into reality.

We have solutions. And if, by chance, we don't, we will find them for you.
