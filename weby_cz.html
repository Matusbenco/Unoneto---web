Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to our clients’ most common questions and concerns

Will I have access to the website administration?

No, and that is an advantage for you. Our model is built on "Hassle-free Web" service. You don't have to learn a new system, deal with updates, or worry about breaking something.

Need to change text, pricing, or a photo? Just write to us, and we'll implement it promptly. You focus on business, we handle the tech.

What is the commitment and setup fee?

You have two options:

  • Yearly payment (RECOMMENDED): Pay for a year in advance, and the setup fee is FREE (€0). You save €199.
  • Monthly payment: Pay a one-time setup fee of €199. Then pay a monthly fee with no long-term commitment (1-month notice period).
Does the €89 price include ad credit?

No. The €89 price covers our work – setting up, managing, and optimizing ads using AI tools. The ad credit (budget) spent on Google or Facebook is paid directly by you to these platforms via your card.

Why should I pay monthly instead of buying once?

Because a website isn't a one-time thing. Tech, Google, and trends change. With a monthly plan, you get:

  • Zero upfront investment (save hundreds at the start).
  • Certainty that the web won't become technically obsolete.
  • A partner who cares for the web (hosting, domain, changes included).
How fast do you implement changes?

Standard requests (text edits, image swaps, opening hours updates) are usually done within 24 to 48 hours on business days. With the Partner package, your requests have priority.

Who owns the domain (.sk / .com / .eu)?

The domain is always registered to you or your company. You are its 100% legal owner. We only technically manage it during our cooperation. If you decide to leave, the domain remains yours.

Can I buy the website as a one-off?

Yes. If you don't want to pay monthly, you can use our configurator and pay for the web once. In that case, you own the code, but future updates, hosting, and content changes are up to you (or you can order them from us as a paid service).
