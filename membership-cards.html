Membership cards offer a modern solution for fitness centers, wellness salons, trampoline parks, coworking spaces, and more.

How does it work in practice?

The process is simple and intuitive:

  • Creation and Setup: Easily create a card in the admin panel with different membership types (e.g., "One-Day Pass," "Monthly Membership") and set time or daily limits.
  • Customer Registration: The customer scans a QR code, and the card is automatically saved to their Apple Wallet or Google Pay.
  • Membership Activation: On their first visit, staff scans the code, selects the membership type, and activates it upon payment. The card in the phone updates immediately.
  • Using the Membership: On subsequent visits, they just scan the card. The system automatically verifies its validity and records the entry.

Case Study: Jump Fun Center

The Jump Fun trampoline center has already successfully implemented this new feature and uses three types of cards to meet the needs of different customer groups:

  • Loyalty Card: Collecting stamps for each entry, with a free reward after ten visits.
  • Training Pass: Prepaid entries at a better price for regular visitors.
  • Weekly Pass: A family package with unlimited access for the entire week.
