DigiSign + Raynet CRM = Maximum Automation

As proud Raynet CRM partners, we see the greatest strength in integration. Imagine this scenario:

  1. Your salesperson moves a business deal to the "Won" stage in Raynet.
  2. The system automatically generates a contract using data from the CRM.
  3. This contract is immediately sent via API to the client for signature through the DigiSign platform.
  4. Once signed, the document automatically returns and attaches to the client's card in Raynet.

The entire process is seamless, flawless, and requires no manual work. This is exactly the efficiency we at UNONETO bring to our clients.
