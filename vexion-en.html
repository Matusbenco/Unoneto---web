Forget plastic cards and complicated apps. Reward your customers the modern way—simply and effectively—right on their mobile devices.

Why Is It Time for Change?

Traditional loyalty programs are inconvenient. Customers lose cards and don't want to install yet another app. Business owners lack access to crucial purchasing behavior data.

Vexion delivers a solution that benefits both sides.

How Does It Work? 3 Simple Steps

1

One Scan

It all starts with scanning a unique QR code at your location. No app downloads, no complicated forms.

2

Instant Registration

Customers enter just basic information and within 40 seconds have the card added to their mobile wallet (Apple Wallet or Google Pay).

3

Collecting Rewards

In a clear interface, you can easily add points, stamps, or grant discounts to the customer. Loyalty has never been easier.

Choose the Right Program for You

We offer 5 types of cards, so you can find a tailor-made solution for your business—from a simple stamp card to season passes.

Stamp Card

Customers collect digital stamps and receive a special reward after reaching the set number.

Points Card

Reward customers with points for every purchase. Later they can redeem them for various rewards, discounts, and benefits.

Discount Card

Flexibly configurable system offering different discount levels based on purchase amount or customer loyalty.

Coupon Card

Use one-time coupons to attract new customers and provide unique discounts, rewards, and benefits.

Membership Card

Time-limited access with a usage limit.
