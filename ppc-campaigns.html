Frequently Asked Questions

Who will manage my campaigns?

We collaborate with a vetted external senior specialist with 10 years of experience. This gives you expertise at the level of a large agency for significantly less money, but with a more personal approach to achieving the best possible results.

How much do I need to invest in ad spend?

This depends on your segment and competition. During the analysis (step 3), we will prepare a budget estimate needed to achieve relevant results. You pay for credit directly to Google/Meta; you pay us only for management.

Do I have access to my accounts?

Of course. The accounts are always your property. We work in them as managers. You have a 100% transparent overview of every cent spent and can see what is happening in the account at any time.
